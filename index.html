Our Services

A wedding is a milestone in your life, a most important and beautiful day for you and your loved ones, a day to celebrate and cherish, so we give you a chance to live and enjoy each and every moment of your wedding day by taking all your responsibilities on us and providing you with the best quality of service, and making your day the most memorable one. We provide all types of services from invites, venues, decoration, theme planning, sangeet, DJ, catering and all other things related to wedding so that you can just enjoy your grand moment freely. We ensure that our clients get superior service and great value for money to celebrate their events with elegance and class. These are some of the services :

Venue Selection

Wedding day is a special day and so is a memorable day in ones life that needs a perfect venue that suits your wedding. We provide you with well equipped halls, a better place to perform weddings as per your requirements so that your comfort and heritage is taken into consideration. Depending upon the expected gathering, budget and other related things we help you to finalise a perfect and appropriate venue for your wedding, which will give you and your guests an extremely wonderful experience.

Designer Invitations

A fabulous invitation is always the life of the wedding. The most glamorous way to invite is to send the most unexpected invite. So for that we bring you the most innovative, creative, and designer invitations. The perfect beginning to your dream wedding is the perfect invitation card.

Decoration and Design

Decorations are the prime factor for the rise and downfall of the pride of a wedding. So for a successful wedding we provide you with a variety of designs and creativity for the decorations. We offer you with a wide range of creative designs and themes to make a wedding look lavishing and gorgeous. But sometimes the bride have her own dreamy design for decoration, so for that we can even make creative designs according to the client requirements.

Catering

In our Indian traditional weddings the foods have been the trade mark of the wedding royalty. Photography and Videography

Photos and videos are the only medium for you to get back to those beautiful days of your life when someone became the most important part of your soul and to cherish it gracefully we provide you with the best photographers and cinematographers which will deliver you with a high quality of service. We capture your once in a lifetime, priceless moments in such a way that each time you see it, it will take you back to that wonderful time.

Mehendi

Indian weddings have many rituals and mehendi is one of the most important among them. And we all know that the beautiful dark mehendi in the hands of bride shows the deepness of love between the couple. So knowing its importance, we provide you with the best mehendi artist from the industry, who can fill your hands with their creativity and make a beautiful design and a sign of a long lasting love.

Sangeet

Sangeet is one of the most important pre-wedding function which gives a chance to family members and friends to show thier love and care by making a healthy participation by performing skits and dance to portray the couple's life journey. Sangeet is a special occasion for the couple to enjoy the light moments and keep those moments alive forever in them. And to cherish it, we take care of all the arrangements.

India has now emerged as a popular Wedding Destination for couples who want thier wedding to be most unique and auspicious one. Having a wedding away from your home town is something your guests will always remember. And it will be a memorable weekend away with your closest freinds and family member. And one should remember that planning a destination wedding is not an easy task but we at EventSIA will make it easy and stress free for you by taking all your responsibilites and we assure you that these will be the most beautiful days of your life.

Our Packages

Love is a beautiful thing to happen. And marriage is its first step. So to make your first step easy and without stress, we provide you with our 4 packages. You can choose the one according to your requirements.

Complete wedding planning

In this package we cover each and every aspects of your wedding. From SAGAI to BIDAI, we take care all your responsibilities, so that you can have a stress free experience. We will be there with you, for you, throughout your wedding period. Selection of vendors to on day hospitality, we will take care of each and everything.

Selective wedding planning

In this package we will only cover the aspects which you want us to do. Like if you have already started your planning for your dream day and booked few of the vendors, but still confused about the other vendors and many more stuff, then this package is for you. The service selection will be according to you and your requirements. Here you can select from minimum service to maximum service.

A week before coordination

When we say a week before your wedding, it means all your work is done. That is from selection of venue to selection of band baja for baratis, everything you have done. So if you feel like now you should only enjoy and have fun in each of the function which takes place before wedding (mehendi, sangeet) so this package is for you. From here onwards we take all your duties and stress. We coordinate with each and every vendor you have booked and take care of all the things till the end.

Wedding day coordination

Here we let you enjoy your dream day freely, without any stress and anxiety about the work. In this package we coordinate with each and every vendors you have booked and see to it that everyone arrives on time on the wedding day. We confirms that everything is done from decoration to catering part. Here we see to it that your wedding runs smoothly thorugh out the day without any problems.
